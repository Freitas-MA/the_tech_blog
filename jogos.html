The last of us - Part II

Publicado em 30/01/2023

The Last of Us is an action-adventure survival horror video game developed by Naughty Dog and published by Sony Computer Entertainment. The game follows the story of Joel and Ellie, two survivors in a post-apocalyptic United States, as they journey across the country in search of a cure for the infection that has devastated the world. The Last of Us is considered one of the greatest video games of all time, and has won numerous awards for its story, characters, and gameplay.

    The Pros:
  • Highly praised story and characters, with emotional and engaging narrative.
  • Challenging and intense gameplay, with a focus on stealth, strategy, and resource management.
  • Incredibly detailed and immersive environment, with stunning visuals and sound design.
  • Excellent replay value, with New Game+ and multiplayer modes.
    The Cons:
  • Limited character customization options.
  • Linear level design, with no real exploration or side quests.
  • Combat can be difficult and punishing, especially on higher difficulty settings.
  • Limited online multiplayer content.

Cyberpunk 2077

Publicado em 30/01/2023

Cyberpunk 2077 is the highly anticipated action-adventure RPG game from CD Projekt Red. The game is set in the futuristic, dystopian world of Night City, and features a deep and engaging storyline full of choices and consequences.

    Pros:
  • Cyberpunk 2077 features an incredibly detailed world, with stunning visuals and an immersive atmosphere.
  • The deep, branching storyline gives players the chance to make their own decisions, and the consequences of these choices can have a major impact on the game’s narrative.
  • The game features a vast array of weapons and customization options, allowing players to create their own unique character and playstyle.
  • The game also features an innovative multiplayer mode, which allows players to team up and explore the world together.
    Cons:
  • Cyberpunk 2077 has been the subject of significant delays, and the game’s release date has been pushed back multiple times.
  • The game has also been the subject of some controversy, with some criticizing its depiction of certain topics.
  • The game has also been criticized for its lack of optimization on certain platforms, particularly the Xbox One and PlayStation 4.
  • While the game features an extensive selection of weapons and customization options, some players may find the selection to be too limited.
